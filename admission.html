    • ADMISSION PROCEDURE

      WALK-IN ADMISSION
      This is the easiest procedure to ensure enrollment in a particular course. The candidate is required to visit The Gems Tuition center with a properly filled application form along with course fees and the required documents mentioned in the application form. If the candidate is found to be fulfilling all the eligibility criterias then he/she will be immediately enrolled to the course.

      ON-LINE ADMISSION
      Candidate can email us the properly filled application form with photocopy of required documents for admission. But he/she needs to submit the fees at our center to secure his/her admission. Fill up the registration form and email us OR
